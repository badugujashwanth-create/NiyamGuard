# Demo Script

1. Open `http://127.0.0.1:5173/demo`.
2. Explain the seeded scenario: GO-138 changes Income Certificate validity from 12 months to 6 months.
3. Click `Run Compliance Demo`.
4. Open the admin portal and sign in with `admin@niyamguard.local / Admin@12345`.
5. Show `/admin/compliance`: MeeSeva portal, officer SOP, and FAQ drift while the simplified form is compliant.
6. Show `/admin/conflicts`: old GO-112 conflicts with GO-138.
7. Show `/admin/reports`: export compliance CSV.
8. Show `/admin/audit`: login, compliance, conflict, and report actions are logged.
9. Open the citizen portal and start Income Certificate.
10. Ask `income certificate validity entha`; confirm GO-138 and 6 months appear in the verified source card.
11. Ask `scholarship documents enti`; confirm a sourced citizen knowledge answer appears.
12. Close by stating the app guides citizens and government teams but does not submit real applications or replace official verification.
