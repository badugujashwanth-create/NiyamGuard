# Privacy Checklist

- Use synthetic data for demos and hackathon judging.
- Do not ingest Aadhaar, phone, or bank data into the RAG index without redaction.
- Keep evidence metadata minimal and purpose-bound.
- Return "not found in available dataset" instead of guessing missing personal or regulatory facts.
- Restrict audit export to admin/reviewer users.
- Define retention and deletion rules before a real pilot.
